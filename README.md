 Project Overview
This project performs a complete financial analysis of Nykaa (FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd.) — one of India's leading beauty and lifestyle e-commerce companies — using real financial data sourced from Screener.in.
The analysis covers 8 years of financial data (FY2018–FY2025), examining revenue growth, profitability, asset growth, and cash flow trends. Python was used for data processing and chart generation, and Power BI was used to build the final interactive dashboard.

📊 Analysis & Visualizations
Python Charts (matplotlib)
Four key financial charts were created:

📊 Revenue Growth (₹ Crores) — Consistent growth from ~₹28Cr (FY2018) to ~₹420Cr (FY2025)
💰 Net Profit (₹ Crores) — Turned profitable from FY2020 onwards, peaked at ₹121Cr in FY2024
🏦 Total Assets Growth (₹ Crores) — Massive jump post-IPO in FY2022, reaching ₹1,850Cr+ by FY2025
💵 Cash Flow Trend (₹ Crores) — Operations vs Net Cash Flow comparison across 8 years

Power BI Dashboard
An interactive dashboard combining all financial metrics with slicers and visual storytelling.

🔍 Key Insights
🚀 Revenue grew 15x from FY2018 to FY2025, showing consistent year-on-year growth
📉 Nykaa reported a net loss in FY2018 but turned profitable by FY2020 and has remained profitable since
🏦 Total assets surged dramatically in FY2022 — coinciding with Nykaa's IPO in November 2021
📈 FY2024 was the most profitable year with net profit of ₹121 Crores
⚠️ Cash flow from operations remained negative for several years, indicating heavy reinvestment phase during growth
💡 FY2025 showed the strongest operational cash flow — a sign of improving business efficiency
